About the Filmmaker

Bio

Mohammed Benabdallah Belbaraka, often called "Benski" to stand out from his common given name, is a senior Computer Science student at Al Akhawayn University. While his main focus is on computer systems, he also pursues a minor in Communication Studies to fuel his deep love for art, filmmaking, and everything behind the camera.

After spending 17 years growing up in Saudi Arabia before moving back to Morocco, Benski used to be quite introverted. Over time, he found his footing and has grown into a highly outgoing person who loves to socialize and connect with new people. Above all, he values his family. While he has an older brother, he shares a particularly unbreakable bond with his twin sister, and he is incredibly proud of her.
